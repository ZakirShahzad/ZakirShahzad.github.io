Website Evaluations

Worst Website

This Website does not follow the principles of C.R.A.P.

  • C - Constrast: The elements of the site is all over the place. We have random things Highlighted and the contrast of white and random colored text does not help with visiability
  • R - Repetition: The Website has around 50 items on which makes it hard to read.
  • A - Alignment: This sites has no alignment. Everything is messy resulting in elements to be overlapping and chaotic.
  • P - Proximity: There are many items for sale in very close proximity where it is hard to tell which price/item belongs to which image.

Good Website

This is a Good example of C.R.A.P.

  • C - Constrast: The contrast for this website has a simple white background with a gold theme with some lighter gradients. It is full of animation and allows the products to stick out.
  • R - Repetition: The repition is very minimal.
  • A - Alignment: The Alignment uses a lot of sections that prompts the user to scroll down the page.
  • P - Proximity: As you scroll down the page, it gets into some products they sell which fits the overall theme of the website. The top menu is simple condensed down to 5 buttons..
