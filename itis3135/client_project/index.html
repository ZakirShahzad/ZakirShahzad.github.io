Our Message

The mission of the UNCC MDL is to provide an environment that empowers all Muslim students in UNC Charlotte spiritually, academically, socially, and professionally by building a bridge between the Muslim community at UNCC and the MDL. ⛏

We will achieve this by, increasing Islamic awareness in the society at large, providing spiritual events, and educational opportunities.

- UNCC MDL Board
